Asian Home Deco (AHD) is the department of KNG Asia, that specializes in helping buyers to select, purchase or produce, consolidate and export classic Chinese furniture and decorations. 

The website www.asianhomedeco.com , and it's founder Siri are famous amongst to professional buyers. Read about the history on the right side of this page.

Asian Home Deco: Decorations for your home in Asian style.

AHD used to be mainly a sourcing service supplier, where Siri was kind of your purchasing agent in China. It has developed in the past years by offering much more than only sourcing services, and we now  also have our own warehouse with some collections and stock of our own. Thanks to the many years of experience we can produce at the very source any model of classic Chinese furniture you require.

We basically advise you the products your shop needs,
get them and the best prices and ship them to you in full containers.

IMPORTANT: we apply a LOWEST PRICE GUARANTEE! That means that we promise that you'll get the lowest price possible for a piece at a certain supplier under the same conditions; in case it's proven differently, we'll pay back the difference! (we have agreements with our main suppliers that they must give us best prices)

The services from AHD are:

  • sourcing and buying
  • price negotiating (we actually guarantee the best price)
  • quality control
  • professional photo shooting
  • (special) packaging, maximized stuffed container, import certificates, ....
  • white-page service: online stock from many warehouse made available through your own website

The history
KNG Asia, the holding company above www.furniture-hq.com and www.asianhomedeco.com , actually found it's roots in Asian Home Deco.
It is Siri Dyal Khalsa, who started back in the year 2000 to work as an agent for buyers in China. Having a passion for furniture and being perfectly bilingual (Chinese and mother tongue American), made him the most wanted help.
That experience and the entrepreneurial mindset brought him to setup KNG Asia, together with French partner Antoine, who was seeking to run a trading and consulting company.

Only in 2011 the department Furniture-HQ was added, when Frederic, the third partner (, joined to create his import project, bringing western deco brands to China.

Everything together makes that today you can buy from KNG Asia a fully mixed container of Chinese antiques and reproductions, modern China-manufactured furniture, and imported ethnic styles from rugs till French Armoires.

Please visit www.furniture-hq.com  for more info.
